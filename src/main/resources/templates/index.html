Indian history is as old as time. Recorded history tells us around 1500 B.C., Aryan tribes originally from the area between the Caspian Sea and Hindu Kush mountains migrated to Indian subcontinent, bringing prototype of the Sanskrit language and culture which have been part of Hindu tradition ever since.


At History Travels, we have tried to bring this richness out to you with curated visiting sites, historian guided tours and taste of local cuisine and handicrafts.
