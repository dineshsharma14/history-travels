This museum was built by the State of Tamil Nadu in 1984 to celebrate the 1000th birth anniversary of the great Chola king Raja Raja Chola. King Raja Raja Chola was the most famous King in the Chola dynasty that ruled Thanjavur.

He was respected and loved for his promotion of art and culture, royal endeavours, efficient administration and feared for a myriad of military conquests. It was King Raja Raja Chola who raised the status of the Chola dynasty to the unexpected heights and founded the way on which the dynasty flourished, grew and spread across the South of India for two long centuries.
